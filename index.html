Tony's Incredible Chili Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound italian sausage)
  • 8 oz chorizo
  • 1 can (20 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (10 oz) Fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (10 oz) Hot diced tomatoes with green chilies
  • 1 can (8 oz) White chili beans
  • 1 can (8 oz) Red chili beans
  • 1 box carol shelbys chili seasoning mix
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon lemon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Everything but elote seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions, cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef and sausage. remove and drain excess fat if necessary.
  2. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and chorizo and cook for another minute until fragrant. Add beef and sausage back to pot
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, beans, chili mix and seasonings.
  5. Add broth and bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and let it simmer uncovered for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add stick of butter.
  8. Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot with your choice of toppings.

Enjoy your meal!
